Membership of Amikaro costs:

  • $15.00 for an individual (1 year)
  • $25.00 for an individual (2 years)
  • $30.00 for a family (2 adults + children under 18 now) (1years)
  • $50.00 for a family (2 adults + children under 18 now) (2years)

How to pay:

Copy the text below into a new email, complete it and send it to amikaro@esperanto.org.au with the subject line “Membership application“ or ”Membership renewal”.


"I would like to (become a member/renew my membership) of Amikaro. Here are my details.
Name:
Email address:
Postal address:
Telephone number(s):
(For new members) How I heard about Amikaro:
I’m applying for membership for: myself/for my family (Delete what doesn’t apply) for ___ years
I intend to pay $___ by:
– Credit card via PayPal (in this website)
– Direct deposit into the bank account of Esperanto Federation of NSW"
