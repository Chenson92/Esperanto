What Is Esperanto?

Esperanto is a language designed to enable easy communication between people of different countries and cultures. It was launched in 1887 by Dr Ludvik Zamenhof under the pseudonym “Doktoro Esperanto” (which literally means ”Doctor Hoper”)

Introduction

Amikaro means " a group of friends" in Esperanto. The mission of Amikaro is to create and maintain a social network that provides a platform for users of all languages worldwide to meet, learn, and practice Esperanto.
